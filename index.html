About Us

W3 initiative is a Ghanaian based non-governmental organization led by girls for girls to girls which empowers young women with skills and girls to break cultural limitation to achieve their goals. We enhance the capacity building of women and youth in Ghana through our vocational Education training (VET), leadership, Technology and entrepreneurship.

We address unemployment, inequality and promote education in Ghana by producing quality educational materials. We are committed to serving women, youth and children by creating project that advance their social and economic welfare.

Specially designed programmes and activities through collaborative efforts with partners and other institutions to reduce the effect of gaps and constraints at current within the Ghanaian economy and empowering citizens particularly women and the youth through various skills development and enhancements programmes.

The project in the short term looks at addressing the challenge of capacity building and job creation for individuals particularly women and the youth whiles further attempts are made in the medium and long term to address other challenges facing the development of SMEâ€™s for women.

OUR VISION

To empower the next generation of African women to be economically independent, break barriers and change the narrative

OUR MISSION

Is to become the leading NGO in Ghana and Africa at large, campaigning for sustainable women empowerment and the development of the youth to build capacities and create sustainable income models through Technical and Vocational Education and Training.

Our Core Values

The Three W(W3)

Women Empowerment as a tool for Economic development

Women Education and Training for sustainability

Women Financial Freedom for financial independence of families
